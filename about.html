About Me

I am a Data Analyst with over two years of experience in the construction, technology, and education industries. I possess extensive expertise in business, data, and financial analytics, with proficiency in Python, Power BI, Tableau, and SQL. When I am not generating insights from big data, I am dedicated to tutoring and mentoring a group of professional data analysts.

I earned my first degree in Electrical/Electronics Engineering and then embarked on an exciting journey with one of Africa's leading construction firms. However, my curiosity and passion for innovation led me to dive into the dynamic field of Information Technology. Working with data has vastly expanded my horizons and continuously fuels my curiosity. I am thrilled by the power of data to uncover insights and tell compelling stories.

Data analytics to me is like working on an electrical design; writing codes to automate processes makes me appreciate my engineering background even more. It also feels like opening up control panels to troubleshoot. I love working behind the scenes of major business decisions, knowing I play a significant role in the decision-making process, which is based not on mere opinions but on metrics(because numbers don't lie) and insights.
